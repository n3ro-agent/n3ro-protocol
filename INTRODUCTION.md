## How n3ro Works

### 1. Scan human behavior at scale.

N3ro continuously scans 24/7 for contract addresses mentioned on X by 20,000+ crypto KOLs. Each KOL is dynamically scored by real-time shill quality, using live trading performance and historical market impact - ensuring early signals come from proven sources, not noise.

### 2. Detected contract

For each detected contract, N3ro evaluates the project’s X activity by analyzing follower quality, active mentioners, mention volume, and engagement growth rate to determine its market awareness stage.

### 3. On-chain analytic

N3ro evaluates comprehensive on-chain token data to assess the strength of the on-chain setup, including contract risk, liquidity, market cap, smart holder presence, creator history, and developer actions.

### 4. Custom skill

Powered by specialized degen trading skills, N3ro converts validated signals into high-probability entries and precise exits, executing autonomously to maximize profit efficiency.
